Hi, I'm the author of this blog, Bea.

This space is where I share my ideas and thoughts about various things. 

I'm an avid reader of fantasy, historical, romance, and mystery novels and webnovels.

I also watch a lot of Chinese, Korean and Japanese dramas.

I also listen to different kinds of music ranging from popular english songs to k-pop and OSTs of dramas that I like.

This little essay on myself is all to give you a sneak into what the future content might be, because I love to dive deep into the little things portrayed and symbolized in these - immersing myself into the beautiful world created by these fictionalities and forgetting all the worldly woes.
